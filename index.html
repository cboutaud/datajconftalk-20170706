Berret & Phillips, "Teaching Data and Computational Journalism", (2016) has four key areas of Data and Computational Journalism Teaching. You can easily get to the first two with some basic 'in-house' j-school coding teaching, and might stretch to the third, but the fourth is unreachable without a solid foundation of CS learning that you are not going to get without CS involvement in the course.
